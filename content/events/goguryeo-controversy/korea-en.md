Goguryeo (37 BC - 668 BC) was an ancient state that existed for approximately 700 years from the northern part of the Korean Peninsula to northeastern China (Manchuria).

According to Korean historical recognition, Goguryeo is a nation founded by the ancestors of the Korean people, and is considered to form the basis of Korean history, along with Gojoseon, Baekje, and Silla. Korean textbooks use the ``Three Kingdoms Period'' (Koguryo, Baekje, and Silla) to teach Goguryeo as an integral part of Korean history.

In 2002, South Korean society strongly opposed China's ``Northeast Project'' project, which positioned Goguryeo as China's ancient regional government. The Korean side refutes this using the following arguments.

The first is **ethnic continuity**. The founder of Goguryeo, Jumong, was of Buyeo descent, and the people of Goguryeo are ethnically and culturally connected to the people of the later Goryeo and Joseon dynasties. After the fall of Goguryeo, many of its survivors joined Silla and Balhae and directly contributed to the formation of the Korean nation.

Second, there is the **succession relationship with the Goryeo Dynasty**. Goryeo, founded in 918, took its name from the word ``Koguryo'' and recognized itself as the legitimate successor of Goguryeo. The current English name "Korea" also originates from Goryeo.

Third, **criticism of the political use of history**. South Korean scholars and the government have criticized China's Tohoku Project, saying that it is not pure academic research, but rather a political and territorial preparation for future changes in the situation on the Korean Peninsula (such as unification issues).

In 2004, the Korean government established the Goguryeo Research Foundation (later reorganized as the Northeast Asian History Foundation), which systematically promotes research to academically prove that Goguryeo belongs to Korean history. Furthermore, North Korea also registered the Goguryeo Ancient Tombs as a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 2004, and both North and South agree that Goguryeo is part of the history of the Korean people.
